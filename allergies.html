foods i am allergic to

i have pollen food allergy syndrome, which means that i am allergic to certain pollens and consequently have an allergic reaction to fruits/vegetables/legumes that are within the same family.

cross-contamination is not an issue , not eating the foods is enough for me to avoid a reaction. i am able to eat (most of) these foods if they are cooked as it destroys the proteins that my immune system reacts to.

DO NOT TAKE ANY OF THIS AS GENERAL ADVICE OR INFORMATION THAT APPLIES TO EVERYONE WITH FOOD ALLERGIES (OR EVEN PFAS). everyone's allergic reactions are different, and some reactions can be deadly, so it's important to learn each person's unique food restrictions.
